Services

Care for garments, linens, and interiors

A broad set of cleaning services, with processes tailored to specific items.

Casual wear cleaning

Everyday garments are gently processed and finished so “your ready-to-wear is ready to go,” with spot removal, environmentally friendly solvent or controlled wet cleaning, and hand finishing.

Perc-free process eliminates the typical dry-cleaning smell.

Evening dresses

Formal gowns are inspected and hand-spotted for oils, makeup, food, or drink stains, then re-inspected and hand-pressed with attention to embellishments, lace, ruffles, sequins, and bias-cut sections.

Fine-hand laundry

“By Hand” care for shirts, bed linens, tablecloths, and fine dry-cleaned items—combining computer-controlled wet cleaning, custom vat soaking, hand spotting, and custom chemistry for crisp, fresh results.

Heirloom restoration is available.

Shirt cleaning

Commercial laundering with special detergents and professional pressing for cleaner collars and crisp finishing, with “by hand” care for custom-made, couture, or designer shirts.

Finish options: hangers or box, with or without starch; buttons replaced automatically.

Bed linens

In-house laundering with professional stain removal and delicate techniques to clean and sanitise while preserving beauty, warmth, and softness; finished with a specialised press, hand ironing, and hand folding.

Table linens

Restoration for fine table linens and family heirlooms using computer-controlled wet cleaning, custom vat soaking, and specialised chemistry, with careful handling of appliques, embroidery, and lace.

Flat board returns or hand-folded packaging with high-quality white tissue.

Interior cleaning (area rugs & drapery)

Area rugs: assessment, detailed inspection, vacuuming, pre-spotting and pre-conditioning; rotary brush shampooing, extraction, and fibre grooming; then thorough drying and protective wrapping.

Drapery: environmentally friendly agents and finishing to keep colours bright; large-capacity machines for any size panels.

Gown preservation

Bridal gowns are inspected for spots or damage, hand-spotted with specialised formulas, cleaned individually, inspected again, then hand-pressed and allowed to hang for 24 hours before being placed in an acid-free chamber for long-term preservation.

Additional services

  • Pick-up & delivery — scheduled pick-up/drop-off; staff drivers only; privacy maintained.
  • Quilts & bedspreads — dry or controlled wet cleaning; restores shape and fluff; pet-stain removal.
  • Repairs & alterations — hemming, zipper replacements, sleeve adjustments, cuff repairs, seam mending (fittings not on-site).

How it works

A discreet, dependable routine

Designed to make customers’ lives easier through convenient pick-up and delivery and detailed, personalised service.

  1. 01

    Schedule pick-up & drop-off

    Route personnel set up pick-up and drop-off times for your home or office.

  2. 02

    Expert cleaning, tailored to the item

    Cleaning may include spot removal, environmentally friendly solvent cleaning, wet cleaning, and hand finishing, supported by state-of-the-art facilities and a strict quality inspection process.

  3. 03

    Finish and presentation

    Items are returned cleaned and expertly pressed; select services include hand folding and protective tissue packaging for storage.

  4. 04

    Delivered back with care

    Staff drivers (no outside services) collect items and return them finished, with customer information kept private.

About

Craftsmanship, modernised

Private Launderers has roots stretching back more than half a century, built on “meticulous care” and “timeless expertise.” Every garment is treated with the utmost care and attention through detailed, personalised service.

With over 50 years of dry cleaning and laundry experience, the team applies modern and traditional fabric-care techniques, using environmentally friendly solvents, computer-controlled wet cleaning machines, or hand cleaning.

Ongoing training keeps staff current with new techniques, fabrics, and fashions—so they can handle whatever clients send.

Quality pledge
